Disease-Predictor

"Disease Predictor" is an example of providing accessible and appropriate health services for all. It diagnoses patients from their symptoms and creates a healthy prognosis system for various diseases, which has evolved from simple to digital prediction. It can be used for heart disease, diabetics, and other common diseases.
