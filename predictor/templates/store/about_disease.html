About App

Disease Prediction, a web application that diagnoses patients from their symptoms, is an example of the latest healthcare construct. Our web application provides simple to digital prediction of various diseases like heart disease, diabetics, etc. This personal health is easily provided with an accessible web interface, it is itself specially customized and able to provide well-informed predictions. It is a modern pro-active health management where urgent recommendations are based on personal parameters and history data. Our system provides a user-friendly web interface to enter personal health information safely and securely. Easy to navigate and personalized forecasts are provided in a clear and understandable format. Be able to easily monitor your health status and receive alerts and updates based on your health information.
